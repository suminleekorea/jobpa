## 3. SOLUTION — What JobPA Does

### EN
**Section Title:** JobPA is your AI career strategist. Not a job board. Not a resume template. A strategist.

**Three pillars:**

**① Know the market before you apply**
JobPA aggregates real-time job data from Career@Gov, MCF, LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, JobsDB, Bayt, GulfTalent, and more — filtered by your target role, experience level, and region. You see what's actually hiring, not what's been posted for 3 months.

**② Know if you're ready before you apply**
Upload your resume. Paste a job description. JobPA's AI gives you a fit score, identifies your gaps, and tells you exactly what to fix — with region-specific advice. Singapore market? Different feedback than Korea or UAE.

**③ Know what to do every single day**
JobPA generates a personalized daily action plan based on your resume gaps, target role, and application pipeline. No more "I don't know what to do today." You wake up with a checklist.

**① Premium Subscription — $29/month**
Unlimited resume analyses, advanced fit scoring, priority job alerts, interview probability engine. Free tier: 3 analyses/month.

**② Consulting Marketplace — 20% platform fee**
Verified career consultants list their services (resume review, interview coaching, visa consultation). JobPA takes 20% per session. Consultants get access to pre-qualified, motivated clients. Target: 500 consultants × $2,000/month average GMV = $200K GMV/month at scale.

**③ B2B Employer Branding — $500–2,000/month**
Companies targeting Korean talent (Singapore MNCs, Korean conglomerates expanding abroad) pay to feature their employer brand, post sponsored job listings, and access aggregated talent insights.
