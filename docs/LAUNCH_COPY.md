## Short Description

## Product Hunt Tagline

Your AI Career Ops dashboard for every job application.

## Product Hunt Description

JobPA is a candidate-side career operating system. Instead of acting like another job board or auto-apply bot, it helps job seekers run the messy middle of the job search: decide which roles are worth applying to, tailor resumes, track applications, prepare interviews, and know the next action.

It is packaged around two products:

- Career Ops Dashboard: application pipeline, fit scoring, resume gaps, follow-ups, and prep tasks.
- AI Career Assistant: career strategy, resume positioning, interview prep, visa context, and escalation to paid consulting when human help matters.
