## DESIGN NOTES FOR SLIDE GENERATION
- **Color palette**: Primary dark #0F172A (navy), Accent #3B82F6 (blue), Success #10B981 (green), Warning #F59E0B (amber)
- **Typography**: Inter or Pretendard — clean, modern, bilingual-friendly
- **Style**: Premium SaaS / startup pitch — not corporate, not casual
- **Slide 1 & 7**: Full dark background for impact
- **Slides 2-6**: Light backgrounds with dark text for readability
- **Images**: Product screenshots, world maps, simple icons — NO stock photos of people
- **Language**: English primary, Korean accent words where impactful (취업비서, 이력서)
- **Max 40 words per slide** (audience listens, not reads)
