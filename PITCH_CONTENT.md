## Slide 3 — The Problem
"Job seekers fail interviews — not because they're unqualified, but because nobody helps them prepare right."

- **200+ avg. applications per hire** → Spray & Pray: Candidates mass-apply without knowing their fit. No feedback loop, weeks wasted on rejections.
- **33min avg. prep time (Indeed, 2025)** → Zero Personalized Prep: Most spend just 33 minutes preparing — studying generic questions, not the actual role or company.
- **24% of Korean top corps use AI hiring screens** → AI Hiring Blindspot: Samsung, Hyundai & SK run AI video + facial analysis. No platform exists to help candidates prepare for this.

## Slide 4 — Solution (3-Sided Marketplace)
"JobPA is a 3-sided marketplace connecting candidates, expert consultants, and hiring companies."

**Candidates** — AI-Powered Prep:
- AI job match scoring
- Personalized prep plan
- Mock interviews + live coaching
- Korea AI hiring simulator

**Consultants** — Earn via Expertise:
- Onboard as verified expert
- Offer paid 1:1 sessions
- Sell prep content packages
- 30% revenue share on all sales (70% to consultant)

**Companies** — Talent Matching Engine:
- AI-matched candidate pipeline
- Pre-screened, interview-ready talent
- Subscription or per-hire pricing
- Reduce time-to-hire significantly

## Slide 5 — Business Model
"3 compounding revenue streams — marketplace network effects grow with every new consultant and company."

Revenue Mix:
- **55% Consultant Marketplace** — 30% take rate. Avg session ₩50K–200K. Target: 1,000 consultants × 20 sessions/mo = ₩1B–4B GMV/mo
- **20% Paid Feedback / Review** — Resume & answer review ₩15K–50K. AI draft + human expert polish. Cover letter, JD-matched resume.
- **18% B2B Talent Matching** — AI-matched candidates for open roles. Subscription or per-hire fee. Pre-screened, interview-ready pipeline.
- **7% Pro Subscription** — Candidate AI tools bundle.

**Year 3 Target: ₩12B ARR**
