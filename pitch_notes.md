## JobPA Context for Pitch

### Founder Story
- Built as a side project (부업) while working full-time
- Personal pain point: navigating job markets across Singapore/Korea/UAE as a Korean professional
- Visa sponsorship complexity, salary benchmarking across currencies, resume localization — all manual pain
- Built JobPA to solve this for the growing community of Korean/Asian professionals seeking global careers

### Problem (Quantified)
- 2.5M+ Korean nationals living abroad (Ministry of Foreign Affairs 2024)
- Singapore: 30,000+ Koreans; 40% on Employment Pass or dependent pass
- Global job market fragmented: LinkedIn (US-centric), Saramin (Korea-only), MCF (SG-only)
- No single platform gives AI-powered, cross-border career strategy in Korean + English
- Resume localization, visa guidance, salary benchmarking = 3 separate painful manual processes

### Solution: JobPA
- AI-powered career strategy partner (NOT auto-apply)
- 5+ AI agents: Resume Analysis, Job Fit, Market Trends, Daily Reports, Career Chat
- Multi-market: Singapore, Hong Kong, Dubai/UAE, South Korea, Remote
- Bilingual: Korean + English
- Real job data: Career@Gov, MCF, JSearch (LinkedIn/Indeed/Glassdoor), Saramin
- Consulting marketplace: book 1:1 sessions with career consultants

### Traction (current)
- Live product at job-pa.com
- Gamification: XP, streaks, badges
- Job journal, mood tracking, daily action plans
- Consulting marketplace with real consultant profiles

### Business Model Canvas
Key Partners: Manus AI (infra), Job data APIs (JSearch/MCF/Career@Gov), Career consultants
Key Activities: AI agent development, job data aggregation, consultant marketplace ops
Key Resources: AI models (Gemini), job databases, user profiles/resumes
Value Propositions:
  - Users: One-stop AI career strategy for cross-border job seekers
  - Consultants: Client acquisition platform with built-in payment
Cost Structure: AI API costs, server infra, marketing
Revenue Streams:
  1. Freemium SaaS: Free tier → Premium ($9.99/mo or $79/yr) — unlimited AI analyses, priority reports
  2. Per-use credits: Resume deep analysis ($2.99), Interview prep session ($4.99)
  3. Consulting marketplace commission: 15-20% of session fee (consultant sets price $50-200/hr)
  4. B2B: Career center licensing to universities/bootcamps ($500-2000/mo)
  5. Future: Visa sponsorship matching fee from employers

### Market Size
- TAM: Global career services market $14.5B (2024), growing 6% CAGR
- SAM: Korean/Asian diaspora job seekers in SG/HK/UAE/AU = ~500K active users
- SOM (Year 1): 5,000 paid users × $9.99/mo = ~$600K ARR
